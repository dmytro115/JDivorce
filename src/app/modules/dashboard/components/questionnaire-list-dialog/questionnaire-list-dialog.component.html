Important Information

  • If you are the victim of domestic violence, then you are the "Petitioner". The person you want protection from and are filing against is the â€œRespondent". The respondent will be given a copy of all the documents you are filing for domestic violence.
  • The filing and outcome of a request for a Domestic Violence Protection Order can have ramifications on other legal proceedings such as family law. Please see a lawyer or an advocate at the county court with more questions.
  • A Domestic Violence Protection Order is just one part of your safety plan. Please consider working with an advocate for additional safety planning.
