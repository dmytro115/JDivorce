Mission

JDivorce's mission is increase access to the justice system using artificial intelligence. Our first accomplishment (2017) is to simplify the divorce process for Washington state both for clients and lawyers.

JDivorce is based in Seattle, and was launched by Microsoft and Amazon alumni."

Benefits for users

JDivorce helps folks seeking a divorce by:

Simplifying: Providing a free do-it-yourself (DIY) website for divorce papers

Transparency: Offering flat rate packages so folks know what they will get and what total price

Convenience: Cutting the difficulty in working with attorneys

Cost-cutting:Offering low prices for hiring quality lawyers (which we do by cutting overheads for lawyers)

Benefits for Professionals

JDivorce cuts expenses for professionals like lawyers, LLLTs, mediators by providing:

  • Website: JDivorce allows legal professionals to setup their website for free on premium .com domains.
  • Marketing: JDivorce is better than a regular "leadgen" because it brings in paying clients. JDivorce brings in real paying clients not by ads but by providing genuinely helpful free online tools such as the divorce document assembly. In addition we do digital marketing on Google Search and Facebook using advanced algorithms to reach the most relevant audience. These are folks who are in more advanced stages of their overall process to hire a professional. You will receive leads for clients in your city.
  • Making Onboarding Easier JDivorce provides initial process and related legal information. So by the time the client reaches you the client has already seen the basic info.
  • Getting Client Information:  When the clients first reaches you, you will also receive their completed online questionnaires that cover all the question on the divorce application. So you can quickly receive all the relevant info.
  • Payments:  When a client hires you, all the payment is paid upfront.
