Affordable flat-rate divorce lawyer

JDivorce lets folks like you going through a divorce to hire lawyers anywhere in Washington state at affordable and flat rate plans. The first step is to figure out the best plan for you. Start by taking the diagnostic test or select from a plan below.

How JDivorce Works

JDivorce uses advanced software to make the process more personal, transparent and economical for you. Key steps are:

  • 1. Select your JDivorce plan

    You can select one of the four plans below. Or if you unsure, just take our diagnostic test. Click begin to complete the friendly questionnaire. JDivorce's web based questionnaires can be accessed from any device: mobile, tablet, laptop. There is no need to download or install any app.

  • 2. Complete diagnostic

    After selecting your plan, you must still complete the diagnostic. JDivorce uses no complicated legal words. The questionnaire is in very simple language aimed at every day folks and not lawyers

  • 3. Get precise legal info

    As you fill your divorce application, JDivorce's online questionnaires provides you with the precise information related to your case. Info (text, videos) on JDivorce is directly provide by a few different lawyers. We aim to provide the same experience to you as visiting a few different lawyers.

  • 4. View your divorce process

    After the initial diagnostic, and even before you purchase a legal plan, you can view the detailed process. Our software takes into account the county you want to file (each county in Washington can have a slightly different set of requirements)

  • 5. Select your lawyer

    Lawyers on JDivorce are qualified attorneys licensed to practice in Washingtons state. You select the lawyer from a list of available lawyers, their first availability, language preference, and their locations. View the total price you will have to pay upfront. There's no hidden fees. You book an appointment with your lawyer as part of the purchase. You can pay via credit card or PayPal

  • 6. Fill your divorce application

    Complete your divorce application online at JDivorce. As you visit different questions, you will see that there's lots of details which will affect your future life. So next to any question or section which is unclear, you can leave questions/comments for your lawyer. Your lawyer may answer them before your meeting (and you will receive notifications via e-mail or text, you can select). Or when you connect with a lawyer, your answers can answered efficiently.

  • 7. Meet your lawyer

    You can meet your attorney in person or online. You will begin with a review of case strategy.

  • 8. (The rest of the process depends on your case)

    A divorce where both spouses agree on the terms can end in about three months and cost just the government fees. Whereas in case the spouses disagree, the process can extend to 1-2 years
