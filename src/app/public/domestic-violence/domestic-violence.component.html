How It Works

From the moment you decided to file for divorce, it takes anywhere between 3.5 to 12 months to finalize a divorce.

1 Take Diagnostic (free!)

Test if you qualify for Domestic Violence Protection Order
✔ Answers questions in simple language. No difficult legal terms
✔ View help content and videos prepared by lawyers in Washington
✔ Get a 'YES' or 'NO' answer within 1 minute

2 View Process, Q&A (free!)

Download the petition and the supporting forms
✔ Petition for Order for Protection
✔ Law Enforcement Information Sheet (LEIS)
✔ All applicable forms as listed on Washington Courts

3 Prepare Application

Answer 3-4 questionnaires to prepare these WA court forms
✔ Petition for Order for Protection
✔ Law Enforcement Information Sheet (LEIS)
✔ Confidential Information
✔ All applicable forms as listed on Washington Courts

4 File at Court (free!)

Visit the county court
✔ File the appication to begin the process
✔ Judge will either issue an emergency order there, or at a hearing within 14 days of filing
