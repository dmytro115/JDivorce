Practicing Law in 3 Steps!

Here's how you can go from here

  • Step 1. Create your profile for free

    Provide an overview such as your areas of practice, location, contact info, licenses, and pricing

    Instructions

    1. Click on "Create Account" to register an account
    2. Click on "Setup"
    3. Step through the questions and upload your photo
    4. Click publish to make your profile be publicly visible

  • Step 2. Turn on client acquisition

    This requires subscribing to the premium packge. You will receive 20 new clients monthly in your inbox. You will be charged after you have received the 20 clients.

    Instructions

    1. Click on "Profile" from the left menu
    2. Setup Pricing tab. Set your own prices for unbundled service packages
    3. Setup Appointments tab.

  • Step 3. Welcome your first client

    Start receiving your new client requests directly into your inbox.

    Instructions

    1. JDivorce gets nearly 200 daily pro se users
    2. We offer lawyer services to these clients and list your profile
    3. When clients reach you, they have done the following:
      • Paid for your service
      • Booked a tentative appointments on your calendar
      • Prepared complete dissolution (or domestic violence) applications
      • Viewed the divorce process as it applies to their case
      • Viewed legal information relevant to their caselegal knowledge base
    4. You can accept, ask more questions to the potential client, decline or simply ignore for auto-decline.
    5. Once you accept, you will receive the payment immediately via direct deposit
    6. You can then engage with the client

In summary, JDivorce complying with one of the most comprehensive security standards, the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS).  PCI DSS is a security standard for software that handles customer information.
