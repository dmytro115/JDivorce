JDivorce Inside!

Upgrade your firms website with family law technology from JDivorce

Embed JDivorce's features directly in your own website. Gratify your clients with cutting edge technology. Offer seamless and secure experience.

Gather client data online

JDivorce provides online surveys to gather client information. The surveys are Turbo-tax-styled i.e. in plain language and for every question provide precise legal information, and detailed articles and video Q&A from lawyers. The question, tool tips and every visible text an be customized

Generate Court Forms

JDivorce can populate Washington family law forms with the client data gathered. The forms can be downloaded as Microsoft Word, Adobe PDF or Google Document formats. The court forms are updated weekly to get the latest revisions from individual county clerks. All Washington counties are supported.

Inline Q&A

Clients can add questions for lawyer as they step through the surveys. Later the lawyer (you) can address all questions in one go, and additionally leave more comments. Inline Q&A allow for precise context-specific discussions for efficiency.

Appointment booking

Link your Google calendar so clients can automatically book appointments and designate rules for automated appointment booking. You can customize many aspects such as duration, minimum time between appointments, and minimum advanced required.

Infogrpahics

The answer to your client's question: "how long is it gonna take" can be a beautiful graphic. JDivorce lets users self generate process visualizations based on case characteristics, and the county rules. Visualizations consistse of a series of steps. Each step consists of what needs to be done, who needs to do it, the fees, the duration and the court forms required

SMS Reminders

JDivorce creates a list of reminders for the clients based on their case schedule issued by the court clerks. You can customize, enable/disable these reminders. These provided an added level of service to your clients without any work for the lawyer's staff

All Cases and Upcoming Tasks

JDivorce provides a dashboard where the lawyer can see all in progress cases. These can be then grouped by complexity (e.g. with children or contested). Lawyer can also see all upcoming important dates across all cases.
